Privacy Charter

Effective: December 2025

Our Commitment to Discretion

At Adult Arrangements, we understand the importance of discretion and privacy for our members. This Privacy Charter explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information within our exclusive community of accomplished individuals.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, date of birth, preferences
  • Portfolio Information: Lifestyle interests, biography, engagement preferences
  • Activity Data: Engagement patterns, preferences, and interaction history
  • Verification Data: Identity verification documents (securely stored and encrypted)

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your exclusive membership portfolio
  • To verify member identities and ensure community safety
  • To facilitate compatible introductions between members
  • To provide discreet support and respond to inquiries
  • To enhance our platform and member experience

Data Security & Discretion

We employ enterprise-grade encryption, secure servers, and strict access controls to protect your data. All personal information is stored with military-grade encryption and accessible only to authorized personnel under strict confidentiality agreements.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your portfolio information is shared only with compatible members as part of our introduction service, and only the information you explicitly choose to share.

Your Rights & Control

You have the right to access, correct, or remove your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, contact our discretion team at discretion@adultarrangements.com.

Platform Purpose Statement

Adult Arrangements is an exclusive social networking platform for adults seeking genuine connections and shared experiences. The platform is strictly for social networking and companionship purposes. Any solicitation or explicit negotiation of services for money is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate membership termination.
